About

  • Tawkir Mahmud Alif
    ( Bangladeshi DJ )

    Tawkir Mahmud Alif better known as Tawkir Alif, is a DJ / Record Producer. Tawkir Alif makes Electronic Music. The most popular categories He use for his musics are, Synth, Bass-Heavy EDM, Future BASS, and Groove-Oriented. In behind the sense of Tawkir Alif's music life, is inspired by Alan Walker and Marshmello. The Most popular Release by Tawkir Alif is "Blind Love" , ( Release Date : Apr 28, 2021 ). After releasing "Blind Love" in 2021, Tawkir blowed up in music industry with a new acquaintance . Also, Tawkir is a Computer Programmer. The most trending programming language "JavaScript", is use for his programming.

  • Early Life

    Tawkir Alif ( born June 20, 2005 ) is an English DJ and record producer. By birth, he got the Bangladeshi Citizenship. Tawkir grew up with one siblings, a little sister, Sabrina Ariya , born in Bangladesh. Growing up in the digital world, Tawkir Alif found a Computer, and he was become a Computer Programmer, Software Developer. Tawkir initially had no musical background, however he later thought himself by watching Alan Walker , Marshmello on TV Industry .

  • Career
    2021 Early Career And Blind Love

    In 2019, Tawkir was listening to a song by English-Norwegian DJ Alan Walker and start thinking about creating EDM Music by himself. He started producing his music on his laptop using FL Studio. In Jan 20, 2020, with the help and feedback from his fans online, he began pursuing his music production career and slowly began posting his music to YouTube and Spotify. Starting as a bedroom producer. Tawkir released the track Wish on 28 April 2021. And Few monthes later he release his new Album Blind Love This Album gained attention after its release in The Biggest Music Platform Spotify. It was the first impression for him as an upcoming artists. The tracks "Found Someone" and "Last Night" followed in 2021.
